One of the biggest UMW projects are the Milky Way Peacekeepers, also nicknamed humorously as Peas, named after their headquarters — a green, spherical ship, as well as their logo. The path to becoming a Peacekeeper is extremely rigorous — the brightest children are taken to the Academy at 3 years old. Only the best of the best graduate, and they still have to complete the three-year practical training and pass the tests.
